2013 MSU Zero-Entry Workshop: Computational Science for Biologists
==================================================================

Sep 23 and 25, 2013.

1425 BPS, Michigan State University.

`Etherpad link <https://etherpad.mozilla.org/bxYhBFc2dk>`__

Lecturers: `Titus Brown <mailto:ctb@msu.edu>`__; `Tracy Teal <mailto:tkteal@msu.edu>`__;

TAs: Likit Preeyanon; Elijah Lowe.
